OUR APPROACH

Ideally, We are all equal. However, that's not the case in real life, there exists a lot more disparities than can be noticed. These disparities are caused by the unequitable sharing and distribution of resources to the marginalised groups in our societies. The contributory factors are also a lack of proper education and sensitisation of these marginalised groups, hence the gap. This is where We,THE ZOGHORI FOUNDATION together with our partners,ZOGHORI CHAMPIONS OF HOPE, come in handy seeking to help bridge the existing gap and elevate the lives of such individuals in our socities. We believe in BUILDING & TRANSFORMATION of one another through concerted and unified efforts, planning and prudential management of acquired resources to achieve our mantra of TRANSFORMING A LIFE TO CHANGE THE WORLD

Who is a Zoghori Champion of Hope?

This is an individual or institution who is willing to commit a certain amount of money, either given monthly, quarterly, annually to the Zoghori Foundation
in ensuring a needy situation will have a solution.

Our belief is that there is hope in every young person to succeed in life no matter his or her background,
hence our responsibility to partner with Zoghori Champions of Hope to fulfil their dreams.

How does one qualify to become a Zoghori Champion of Hope?

By either the individual or institution making a commitment to commit a monthly donation of kshs.50 to Kshs.1,000 and above, paid either monthly ,quarterly, half yearly and yearly.

By seeking a donation from kshs.50 is that no one with a heart to touch a needy situation is locked out.

  history

Zoghori Foundation is the vehicle for Zoghori Sacco Society Ltd for Corporate Social Responsibility.

The current flagship project of the Foundation is an education SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME for bright but less fortunate children
who are facing difficulties in paying school fees for both secondary and university education.

Zoghori Foundation was established in 2019 as a registered trust in recognition of the
need to enhance the existing Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives by the society

At that point, the society felt it was its duty to not only grow
financially but also develop a deeper commitment to help enhance the growth of the community

We believe that our role goes beyond profit-making to include promoting the long-term economic
prosperity and quality of life for everyone in our communities

If our people prosper, we prosper as a society and soar to unimaginable heights of achieving our motto
of BUILDING ONE ANOTHER

In the year 2016, Zoghori Sacco Society Limited successfully rolled out an Education program to pay
school fees for needy secondary school students as an integral part of its CSR projects

The program will continue in the days ahead, to enable these children to achieve their full potential

  SCHOLARSHIP

The secondary scholarship program was initiated in the year 2016 and has grown from an initial sponsorship of 1 students in one County to date to Four students, three in secondary schools and one in the University and the fifth recommended and sponsored by Co-operative Bank Foundation courtesy of Zoghori Sacco Society Ltd.

PROCESS OF APPLICATION:

To be considered for scholarship, one has to fill in an application form at one of our branches and submit it within the given time-frames after which the forms go for vetting and successful applicants contacted

  QUALIFICATIONS

One must:

  1. Be a needy child.
  2. Have excelled well in their K.C.P.E exams and earned a position in a government secondary school in Kenya.
  3. Be able to verify the position of need.
